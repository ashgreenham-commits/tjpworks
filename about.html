Our Story

Built on trust,
driven by genuine parts.

TJP Works began with a simple idea — give UK enthusiasts the same access to genuine JDM parts that Japanese builders take for granted. No grey-market guesswork, no inflated markups, no compromises on quality.

At the heart of TJP Works is Tsubasa, our Japan-based sourcing expert with years of deep relationships across Japan's specialist dismantlers, OEM suppliers and aftermarket manufacturers. Tsubasa knows the market inside out — which suppliers are reliable, which parts are genuine, and how to track down the hard-to-find pieces that other importers can't touch.

On the UK side, Ash — a lifelong JDM enthusiast — manages everything from the moment a part leaves Japan. Liaising with customers, handling customs, coordinating fast UK dispatch and providing the kind of hands-on advice that only comes from actually building and running these cars.

Together, the two sides of TJP Works form something rare: a genuinely seamless bridge between Japan's parts ecosystem and the UK's passionate JDM community.

The kind of workshop knowledge that only comes from years in the game.
